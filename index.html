ABOUT

Our purpose is to: help our community learn more computer science by hosting seminars and volunteering to teach code, working across organizations to reach a wider audience, making computer science more accessible and easy to understand, providing scholarship, internship, & volunteering opportunities, and giving recognition to honor students.

To participate in CSHS, you must maintain at least a 3.0 unweighted overall GP and at least a 3.5 unweighted GPA in computer science courses. You must pay annual dues of $35, have good behavioral standing, attend 6 out of 8 of the monthly meetings, attend both guest speaker events, obtain at least 5 hours of computer science volunteering, obtain at least 5 hours of non-MVHS volunteering, and compete in at least one coding competition throughout the school year (Hackathon, TSA, Cyberpatriots, etc).
